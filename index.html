Displacement VR

Displacement VR is a mobile VR game currently in development. It will target Quest 3, 3S and Quest 2, later PCVR and PSVR. It is a first person tower defence / strategy with an extraction twist. Collect resources, build your base and expand, get as much as you can before having to retreat! In between deployments unlock new weapons buildings and upgrades
